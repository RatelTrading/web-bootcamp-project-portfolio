The Best Movies According to Ratel

Our top 3 movies of all-time.

The 5th Element

In the colorful future, a cab driver unwittingly becomes the central figure in the search for a legendary cosmic weapon to keep Evil and Mr. Zorg at bay.

7.6/10: IMDB: The 5th Element

The Usual Suspects

Following a truck hijack in New York, five criminals are arrested and brought together for questioning. As none of them are guilty, they plan a revenge...

8.5/10: IMDB: The Usual Suspects

The Matrix

Neo, a computer programmer and hacker, has always questioned the reality of the world around him.

His suspicions are confirmed when Morpheus, a rebel leader, contacts him and reveals the truth to him.

8.5/10: IMDB: The Usual Suspects
